About Us



A-One Foreign Services is one of the best and distinguished agencies in the field of recruitment of all kinds of manpower(skilled, semi-skilled and unskilled) to work across industries - governmental and private sectors. Our office is approved & licensed by Govt. of India with License No.: (B-0718/DEL/PER/1000+/5/9088/2014).

Vision

To Be A Pioneer & Reliable Consultancy For Sourcing & Recruitment Of Skilled, Semiskilled and Unskilled manpower.

Mission

To Achieve A Highest Competence In Providing Recruitment Solutions For Employers & Employees.

Values

Professional Temperament, Commitment, Transparency & Trust Between Employer, Employees and Us.


Our office is situated at a prime location in the capital city New Delhi which is easily accessible from the international airport, near five-star hotel (The Suryaa), various government departments, foreign embassies & consulates, airlines, and well-known logistic support providers. Our office is fully equipped with all modern facilities for management and communication to pursue the workflow in a fast & continuous manner according to high quality standards.

CEO Message

A-one Foreign Services (AFS) is the brainchild of its founder, who brings over 26 years of experience working in the Saudi Ministry of Health's New Delhi office, under the auspices of the Royal Embassy of Saudi Arabia in India. During his tenure, he held various key administrative positions and successfully executed several high-profile recruitment projects for Doctors and Staff Nurses for the Ministry of Health, Saudi Arabia.

AFS aims to provide job opportunities for Indians to work abroad in core industries, including HealthCare, Oil and Gas, Power and Petrochemicals, Infrastructure Development, Service Industry.

Our objective is to be a trusted partner in the growth of our overseas employers by providing them with a skilled and unskilled workforce, selected based on high-quality standards.

We execute operations and projects efficiently through our core team of highly qualified and experienced staff members, ensuring utmost satisfaction for our foreign employers.

We look forward to building successful relationships with overseas employers and job seekers by delivering exceptional services.

Hamid Ali
Managing Director
